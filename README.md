This portfolio is a reflection of my growth as a 4th-year Computer Science student at the University of Santo Tomas. Beyond just a list of links, it tells the story of how I approach problem-solving—whether I'm building a subcontracting system like SMARTS, developing AI models for my thesis, or designing architectural concepts like the Yugto Tower. ## 🛠️ Technologies Used
To ensure a smooth user experience and easy maintenance, I utilized:

* 🎨 **Frontend:** HTML5 and CSS3 for structure and custom styling.

* ✨ **Interactivity:** JavaScript for dynamic elements and smooth scrolling.

* 🚀 **Deployment:** GitHub Pages for fast, reliable hosting.

* 📈 **Design Tools:** Figma for prototyping and asset creation.

## ✨ Features
* 📂 **Project Gallery:** An organized display of my key works, including technical descriptions and direct links to repositories.

* 📄 **Interactive Resume:** A digital version of my CV highlighting my roles in projects like DreamSnap and SMARTS.

* 📱 **Fully Responsive:** Optimized for viewing on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

* 📬 **Contact Integration:** A direct way for recruiters and collaborators to reach out via email or LinkedIn.

* 🌑 **Thematic Design:** A modern aesthetic that balances professionalism with my personal brand.

## ⚙️ The Process
* 🔍 **Concept & Layout:** I started by mapping out the user journey—ensuring that my most impactful projects (like my AI Thesis) were front and center.

* 📐 **Styling:** I focused on a minimalist design language that allows the project content to speak for itself without unnecessary clutter.

* 🔨 **Development:** I wrote the codebase from scratch to have full control over the animations and the responsiveness of the grid layouts.

* 🚀 **Optimization:** I compressed assets and optimized the code for fast loading times to ensure a professional first impression.
